Margin is the outerspace around the border of an element. It has 4 properties, margin-top, margin-right, margin-bottom and margin-left.

Border is used to give an outline around elements. It is in between the padding and margin, it has properties of style, width, color and radius.

Padding is the space between a content and it's border. It has properties of top, right, bottom and left
