Fried Rice

About Fried Rice

Fried rice is a dish of cooked rice that has been stir-fried in a wok or a frying pan and is usually mixed with other ingredients such as eggs, vegetables, seafood, or meat. It is often eaten by itself or as an accompaniment to another dish. Many varieties of fried rice have their own specific list of ingredients. In Greater China, common varieties include Yangzhou fried rice and Hokkien fried rice. Japanese chāhan is considered a Japanese Chinese dish, having derived from Chinese fried rice dishes. In Southeast Asia, similarly constructed Indonesian, Malaysian, and Singaporean nasi goreng and Thai khao phat are popular dishes. In the West, most restaurants catering to vegetarians have invented their own varieties of fried rice, including egg fried rice. Fried rice is also seen on the menus of American restaurants offering cuisines with no native tradition of the dish.

Nigerian fried rice is made with long-grain rice, protein (such as beef liver, chicken, pork, or shrimp), vegetables (such as carrots, peas, green beans, onions, and chillies), herbs and spices (such as thyme, pepper, and curry powder), and so on. It is mostly prepared during special occassions such as wedding ceremony, child dedication, birthday party and many more.

Ingredients of fried rice

Ingredients Measurements
Rice 3 cups
Vegetables (Carrots,Spring onions, Green peas, Sweet corn, Green pepper) 2 Cups
Onions 1 cup of diced onions
Chicken stock cubes 5 cubes
Salt 2 tsp
Chicken whole chicken
Cayyene Pepper 2 tbsp
Chicken Stock 1 cup
Vegetable Oil 3 tbsp
Curry Powder 2 tsp
Thyme powder 1 tsp
Shrimps (optional) 1/2 cup
Cooked and diced liver (optional) 1/2 cup

Steps in preparing fried rice

  • Wash the rice properly to reduce the starch
  • Heat 2 tablespoons of oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the rice and sauté for 3-5 minutes, until toasted.
  • Add 1 cup of stock to the rice, stir and cook on low heat, with the lid on for 12-15 minutes, or till stock is absorbed and rice has softened a bit. Let cool for 5-10 minutes,
  • In another pot, heat up the remaining 2 tablespoons oil till hot, then add the hard vegetables. Season with a pinch of salt, 2 seasoning cubes and stir fry for 2-3 minutes before you add the soft mixed vegetables. Let it cook, for another 2-3 minutes.
  • Add curry powder, dried thyme, cayyene pepper, turmeric powder and ginger.
  • Once the vegetables are fried, add the rice and stir gently, so the rice and vegetables combine properly. Add 1/2 cup of stock, cover the pot and cook on low-medium heat for 10-15 minutes
  • You can also decide to add sautéd liver and shrimp
  • When rice is ready, remove from heat and serve with fried chicken or any meat of your choice
